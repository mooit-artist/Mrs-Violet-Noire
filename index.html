About Mrs. Violet Noire

For over a decade, I have been immersed in the shadows of literary crime, investigating the finest mysteries from around the world. From the fog-shrouded streets of Victorian London to the sun-drenched villages where secrets simmer, I seek out stories that challenge, surprise, and satisfy.

My passion lies in uncovering not just the plots that enthrall, but the craftsmanship that makes great mystery writing an art form. Whether you're seeking your next page-turner or looking to explore new corners of the genre, I'm here to guide your literary investigations.
